Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions for you and your family

General Medicine

Comprehensive primary healthcare services for patients of all ages. Our general medicine department provides preventive care, health screenings, and treatment for common medical conditions with personalized care plans.

Pediatrics

Specialized medical care for infants, children, and adolescents. Our pediatric team provides comprehensive healthcare in a child-friendly environment, including routine check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment of childhood illnesses.

Cardiology

Advanced cardiac care services including heart disease diagnosis, treatment, and prevention. Our cardiology department offers comprehensive heart health services with modern diagnostic equipment and expert cardiac specialists.

Neurology

Expert neurological care for disorders of the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. Our neurology specialists provide diagnosis and treatment for conditions such as epilepsy, stroke, and neurological disorders.

Orthopedics

Specialized treatment for bone, joint, and musculoskeletal conditions. Our orthopedic department provides comprehensive care for fractures, joint problems, and sports injuries with advanced surgical and non-surgical treatments.

Ophthalmology

Led by Dr. Neema Daniel Kanyaro, our ophthalmology department provides comprehensive eye care including cataract surgery, glaucoma treatment, and advanced vision correction procedures.
